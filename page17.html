Responsibilities in the Community

Responsibilities of the Seigneur

1.) Distribute land to settlers:
Seigneurs divided their land into smaller plots and rented them to the farmers also known as habitants so the land could be settled and farmed

2.) Encourage settlement and development
They were expected to attract settlers and help develop the land by clearing areas for farming and building communities

3.) Build and maintain a mill
Seigneurs had to build a gristmill where habitants could grind their grain into flour.

4.) Help maintain roads and infrastructure
Seigneurs helped organize the construction and maintenance of roads and other local improvments.

5.) Provide local leadership
They often act as local leaders, helping organize community and representing local interests.
