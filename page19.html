Responsibilities in the Community

Responsibilities of the Catholic Church

1.) Provide religious services
The Church organized religious life in communities by holding Mass, baptisms, marriages and funerals.

2.) Guide moral and social life
Priests helped guide the community's values, behaviour, and traditions, and the church was important authority in daily life.

3.) Support Education
The Catholic Church was responsible for many schools, helping teach children reading, writing and religious instruction.

4.) Provide community support
Church institutions helped care for the poor, sick, and elderly through charities, hospitals, and religious organizations.

5.) Collect the tithe
Habitants paid a tithe (about one tenth of their agricultural production) support the Church and local parish.
