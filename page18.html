Responsibilities in the Community

Responsibilities of the Habitants

1.) Pay rebt
Habitants paid annual rent to the seigneur for the land they farmed. This could be paid in money, crops or other goods.

2.) Perform labour for the seigneur
Habitants sometimes had to complete a few days of unpaid labour each year, such as build roads or helping with community projects.

3.) Use the seigneur's mill
Habitants were required to grind their grain at the seigneur's mill and pay a small fee.

4.) Farm and improve the land
They were expected to clear the land, build homes, and farm land productively.

5.) Follow local regulations
Habitants had to respect rules set by the seigneur and the colonial government, including maintaining roads and property boundaries.
