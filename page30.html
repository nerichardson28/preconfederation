Timeline of the Rebellion


Rebellion Starts in 1837 in both Upper and Lower Canada

1834:
- Britain Rejects Papineau’s Nine-Two Resolutions demanding political change
- Things get tense between Britain and Lower Canada

November 1837 - Battle of Saint-Denis:
- Fighting Breaks out between Patriotes and British Forces one of the only victories the rebels had
- Encourages further resistance

December 1837 - Battle of Saint-Eustache:
- British forces defeat the rebels.
- Marks the end of the Lower Canada rebellion
