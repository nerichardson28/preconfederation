Rebellion in the Colonies


William Lyon Mackenzie
- an elected official who wanted reform and democracy for Upper Canada
-In 1824 he launched the Colonial Advocate, a newspaper that attacked the Family Compact
-He was Elected repeatedly to the Assembly of Upper Canada begining in 1828, Mackenzie was expelled multiple times by Tory majorities but continually re-elected by his constituents

- After years of frustration with colonial governance, Mackenzie led a poorly organized uprising against British authority in December 1837. The rebellion failed, forcing him to flee to the United States.
